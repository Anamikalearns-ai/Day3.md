# Day 3 - Role-Based Prompting

## What I Learned
Role-Based Prompting means assigning Claude a specific expert persona before asking a question. Instead of generic answers, you get expert-level responses.

## Prompt Comparison

### WITHOUT Role
**Prompt:** How should I grow my Instagram account?

**Output:** Post consistently, use hashtags, engage with followers, write good captions.

### WITH Founder Persona
**Prompt:** You are a successful founder who grew a brand to 100K followers organically. How should I grow my Instagram account?

**Output:** 1. Niche down hard - Be specific, people follow you not a topic.
2. Post consistently, not constantly - 3-4x per week.
3. Carousels are king - Saves and shares matter more than likes.
4. Hooks are everything - You have 1 second on the feed.
5. Engage before and after posting - 15 mins before, 15 mins after.
6. Use your story authentically - Stories build loyalty.
7. Collaborate early - Even at 200 followers, do collabs.
8. SEO your captions - Instagram is a search engine now.
9. Document more than educate - You don't need to be expert to grow.
10. Track what's working - Every 2 weeks review top 3 posts.

### WITH Developer Persona
**Prompt:** You are a senior software developer with 10 years of experience who grew a tech personal brand to 50K followers. How should I grow my Instagram account?

**Output:** Core Insight: Tech audiences follow proof, not promises.
1. Build in public - Show actual work, not just tips.
2. Make complexity digestible - Translate hard concepts into simple slides.
3. Use code/data as visual hooks - Real screenshots stop the scroll.
4. Create a series format - Week 1 of learning X builds narrative.
5. Carousels with one concept per slide - One idea, max 2 lines.
6. Comment on bigger accounts strategically - Add value, not just great post.
7. Repurpose your learning as content - Every module = content opportunity.
8. SEO-first captions - Write like someone would Google the problem.
9. Engagement pods = strategy - 5-8 genuine creators, same niche.
10. Ship something useful - Free checklist or template in bio link.

## Key Takeaway
Same question + different role = completely different expert answer.
